# CONTENT_GUIDELINES.md — Content Writing Standards

This document defines how all website content should be written.

Every AI agent must follow these guidelines when creating or editing content.

The goal is to maintain a consistent, professional, and trustworthy brand voice across the entire website.

---

# Writing Philosophy

Always write for humans first.

Content should help visitors understand:

- who BBS Conveyor is
- what BBS offers
- why customers should trust BBS
- how BBS can solve industrial problems

Never write content only to satisfy search engines.

---

# Target Audience

Primary audience

- Factory Owners
- Procurement Teams
- Engineers
- Maintenance Teams
- Manufacturing Companies
- Industrial Buyers

Write as if speaking to professionals.

Avoid consumer-oriented language.

---

# Brand Voice

The tone should be

- Professional
- Confident
- Helpful
- Technical
- Trustworthy
- Clear

Avoid sounding

- aggressive
- overly promotional
- exaggerated
- casual

Good

"We provide conveyor solutions designed for demanding industrial environments."

Bad

"We are the best conveyor company in Indonesia!"

---

# Writing Style

Use

- short paragraphs
- descriptive headings
- bullet points
- clear explanations

Avoid walls of text.

---

# Sentence Length

Prefer

10–20 words per sentence.

Mix short and medium sentences.

Avoid overly long paragraphs.

---

# Titles

Titles should be

- descriptive
- concise
- keyword-focused

Good

PVC Conveyor Belt

Conveyor Installation Services

Industrial Roller Solutions

Bad

Welcome

Our Amazing Products

---

# Headlines

Headlines should explain value.

Good

Reliable Conveyor Systems for Modern Manufacturing

Bad

Welcome to Our Website

---

# Product Content

Every product page should answer

1. What is it?

2. What problem does it solve?

3. Where is it used?

4. What are its advantages?

5. Why choose BBS?

6. Call To Action

Recommended structure

## Overview

## Applications

## Features

## Benefits

## Specifications (optional)

## Why Choose BBS

## Request a Quote

---

# Service Content

Every service page should explain

Overview

Business Benefits

Process

Industries Served

Why Choose BBS

CTA

Focus on customer outcomes.

Not internal company processes.

---

# Industry Pages

Industry pages should explain

Industry challenges

Common conveyor problems

Recommended solutions

Relevant products

Contact BBS

Help visitors understand why the solution fits their industry.

---

# Blog Articles

Recommended structure

Introduction

Problem

Explanation

Solutions

Best Practices

Conclusion

CTA

Articles should educate.

Avoid writing sales pages disguised as blog posts.

---

# About Page

Should answer

Who are we?

Our mission

Our experience

Our values

Why customers trust us

CTA

Avoid writing a company history timeline unless necessary.

---

# Contact Page

Include

Office information

Business hours

Phone

Email

Map

Contact form

Call To Action

---

# Homepage

Homepage should answer

Who is BBS?

↓

What products are offered?

↓

What services are available?

↓

Why choose BBS?

↓

Industries served

↓

Featured products

↓

Latest articles

↓

Contact

---

# Product Descriptions

Keep descriptions

Simple

Technical

Useful

Avoid excessive marketing.

Good

"PVC conveyor belts designed for light and medium-duty industrial applications."

Bad

"The world's greatest conveyor belt!"

---

# CTA Guidelines

CTA should feel helpful.

Good

Request a Quote

Talk to Our Engineer

Contact Our Team

View Products

Avoid

Buy Now

Order Today

Limited Time

---

# Lists

Prefer bullet lists.

Example

Applications

- Food Processing
- Mining
- Packaging
- Logistics

---

# Numbers

Use numbers whenever possible.

Good

20+ Years Experience

500+ Projects Completed

Bad

Many years of experience

---

# Images

Images should support the content.

Preferred

- Real products
- Factory installations
- Engineering work
- Warehouse
- Conveyor systems

Avoid

- Generic office workers
- Handshakes
- Fake AI people
- Unrelated stock photos

---

# SEO Writing

Naturally include keywords.

Do not repeat keywords unnaturally.

One primary keyword per page.

Support with related terms.

Never keyword stuff.

---

# Internal Links

Whenever appropriate,

link to

Related Products

Related Services

Related Industries

Related Articles

Example

"This conveyor belt is commonly used in the Food Industry."

Both pages should link to each other.

---

# Formatting

Prefer

## Heading

Paragraph

Bullet List

Image

CTA

Avoid large uninterrupted text.

---

# Reading Level

Write clearly enough for

Engineers

Purchasing Teams

Factory Managers

Operations Managers

Do not assume deep technical knowledge.

Explain technical terms when necessary.

---

# Content Freshness

Products

Review when specifications change.

Services

Review every 6–12 months.

Blog

Publish regularly.

Update outdated information when necessary.

---

# Grammar

Use consistent capitalization.

Use sentence case for headings unless branding requires otherwise.

Avoid excessive punctuation.

Bad

HIGH QUALITY PRODUCTS!!!

Good

High-quality conveyor solutions

---

# Trust Signals

Whenever possible include

- Years of experience
- Certifications
- Industries served
- Completed projects
- Technical expertise
- After-sales support

Show evidence instead of making claims.

---

# Things to Avoid

Do not exaggerate.

Do not use clickbait.

Do not use buzzwords without explanation.

Do not make unsupported claims.

Do not copy content from competitors.

Do not write filler paragraphs.

---

# AI Content Rules

Every piece of generated content should answer

What?

Why?

How?

What's next?

If the content does not help a potential customer make a decision,

rewrite it.

---

# Agent Checklist

Before publishing content verify

✓ Clear title

✓ Helpful introduction

✓ Proper heading hierarchy

✓ Natural keyword usage

✓ Internal links

✓ CTA included

✓ No duplicate content

✓ Grammar checked

✓ Professional tone

✓ Human-readable

Content should always educate first and sell second.
